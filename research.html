Research at CVR College of Engineering

CVR College of Engineering is dedicated to fostering a strong research culture among students and faculty. The institution encourages innovation, interdisciplinary collaboration, and practical problem-solving across multiple domains of engineering and technology.

The college houses several specialized research centers and labs equipped with advanced technology to support cutting-edge research in areas such as Artificial Intelligence, Internet of Things (IoT), Renewable Energy, Robotics, Data Science, and more. Faculty members actively publish in reputed journals and participate in national and international conferences.

CVR actively collaborates with industries and academic institutions to undertake joint research projects that address real-world challenges. Students are encouraged to take part in research projects, internships, and workshops to gain hands-on experience.

The research department also organizes seminars, guest lectures, and symposiums regularly to keep the academic community updated with the latest trends and advancements in technology.

Research Areas

  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
  • Internet of Things (IoT)
  • Renewable Energy Systems
  • Robotics and Automation
  • Data Science and Big Data Analytics
  • Cybersecurity
  • Wireless Communication
