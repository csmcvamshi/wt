Welcome to CVR College of Engineering

CVR College of Engineering, located in Hyderabad, India, is one of the premier engineering institutions in the country. It offers a vibrant academic environment focused on innovation, research, and holistic development. The college is approved by AICTE and affiliated with JNTU Hyderabad. With state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and a strong focus on student success, CVR College continues to be a leader in technical education.

Established in 2001, CVR College is committed to providing quality education and fostering industry collaborations. The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various engineering disciplines including Computer Science, Electronics, Mechanical, Civil, and more. It promotes interdisciplinary research and encourages students to participate in national and international competitions and conferences.

Beyond academics, CVR emphasizes the overall personality development of students by supporting extracurricular activities, sports, and cultural events. The institution’s dedicated placement cell works tirelessly to ensure excellent career opportunities for its graduates with top multinational companies visiting every year.

CVR College strives to nurture future engineers who are socially responsible, innovative, and equipped with skills for the global challenges ahead. Join CVR to be part of a dynamic and inspiring community that shapes tomorrow’s leaders and innovators.
